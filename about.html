Why we started

Gifting that actually means something

We started Gathered Basket Co. because we were tired of receiving — and sending — gift baskets full of things nobody uses. Waxy chocolates nobody eats. Cheap candles that smell like a car freshener. That one cheese spread that comes in every set but ends up in the trash.

So we built something different: occasion-first baskets where every single item earns its place. And we went one step further — if you don't want something, you can take it out and pay less. Because you shouldn't be charged for things you didn't want.

We're based in Springfield, Virginia, and we ship everywhere. Every basket is assembled by hand before it leaves us.
