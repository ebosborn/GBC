How it works

Ordering a custom basket is fast, easy, and you're in control every step of the way.

01

Pick your theme

Browse six occasion-specific themes — New Job, Retirement, Sympathy, Graduation, Teacher Appreciation, and Engagement. Each one is built around what actually makes sense for the moment.

02

Remove what you don't want

Every item in every basket can be excluded. Remove it, and the price drops automatically — no substitutions, no awkward conversations, no filler. You only pay for what's in the box.

03

Add a personal touch

Include a handwritten note, choose a ribbon color, or request custom branding for corporate orders. The details matter, and we'll make sure they land right.

04

We handle the rest

We hand-assemble your basket, wrap it beautifully, and ship it nationwide — or you can do local pickup in Springfield, VA. Every order includes tracking so you know exactly when it arrives.

Common questions

How long does shipping take? ▼
Standard shipping is 3–5 business days. We also offer expedited 1–2 day shipping if you need it fast. Orders typically leave us within 1–2 business days of confirmation.
Can I really remove any item? ▼
Yes — any item can come out. The price adjusts to reflect exactly what's included. You can try this live on the New Job basket using our interactive customizer.
Do you do bulk or corporate orders? ▼
Absolutely. We have clients ordering 20–200 baskets at a time for employee onboarding, client gifting, and holiday programs. Bulk orders get volume pricing and dedicated support. Reach out through the contact page.
Can I add a personalized note? ▼
Yes — every order includes the option to add a handwritten note. Just include your message in the order form and we'll write it out by hand on a card that ships with the basket.
What if I want something not listed? ▼
Send us a message on the contact page. We love special requests and can often accommodate them with a little lead time — especially for large or recurring orders.
