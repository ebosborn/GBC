Our story

Gifting that
actually means something

We started The Gathered Basket Co. because we were tired of generic gift sets full of things people don't use. Waxy chocolates. Cheap candles. That weird cheese spread nobody asked for.

So we built something different — themed baskets with items that are genuinely good, paired with the freedom to remove what doesn't fit. The result is a gift that feels like it was made for the person receiving it. Because it was.
