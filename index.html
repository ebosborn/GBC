How it works

Ordering a custom basket is fast, easy, and you're in control every step of the way.

01

Pick your theme

Browse six occasion-specific themes — New Job, Retirement, Sympathy, Graduation, Teacher Appreciation, and Engagement. Each is built around what actually makes sense for the moment.

02

Remove what you don't want

Every item can be excluded. Remove it, and the price drops automatically — no substitutions, no awkward conversations, no filler. You only pay for what's in the box.

03

Add a personal touch

Include a handwritten note, choose a ribbon color, or request custom branding for corporate orders. The details matter, and we'll make sure they land right.

04

We handle the rest

We hand-assemble your basket, wrap it beautifully, and ship it nationwide — or you can do local pickup in Springfield, VA. Every order includes tracking.

Common questions

How long does shipping take?▼
Standard shipping is 3–5 business days. Expedited 1–2 day shipping is also available. Orders typically leave us within 1–2 business days of confirmation.
Can I really remove any item?▼
Yes — any item can come out and the price adjusts to match. Try it live on the New Job basket using the interactive customizer.
Do you do bulk or corporate orders?▼
Absolutely. We handle 20–200+ basket orders for employee onboarding, client gifting, and holiday programs. Bulk orders get volume pricing and dedicated support.
Can I add a personalized note?▼
Yes — every order includes the option to add a handwritten note. Just include your message in the order form and we'll write it out by hand.
What if I want something not listed?▼
Send us a message on the contact page. We love special requests and can often accommodate them with a little lead time.

Why we started

Gifting that actually means something

We started Gathered Basket Co. because we were tired of receiving — and sending — gift baskets full of things nobody uses. Waxy chocolates nobody eats. Cheap candles. That one cheese spread that ends up in the trash.

So we built something different: occasion-first baskets where every single item earns its place. And we went one step further — if you don't want something, you can take it out and pay less. Because you shouldn't be charged for things you didn't want.

We're based in Springfield, Virginia, and we ship everywhere. Every basket is assembled by hand before it leaves us.
